## The per-seat problem

Lemlist's pricing looks reasonable for one person. $39/mo for Email Starter. $69/mo for Email Pro. $99/mo for Multichannel Expert.

Then your team grows.

| Team size | Lemlist Email Pro | Lemlist Multichannel |
|----------:|------------------:|---------------------:|
| 1 person | $69/mo | $99/mo |
| 3 people | $207/mo | $297/mo |
| 5 people | $345/mo | $495/mo |
| 10 people | $690/mo | $990/mo |

At 5 team members on the Multichannel plan, you're paying $495/mo — just for sending. Add a lead database ($79/mo), enrichment ($149/mo), verification ($15/mo), and infrastructure ($120/mo), and you're past $858/mo.

Per-seat pricing penalizes growth. Every new team member is another $69-99/mo. Flat-rate pricing rewards it.

## What Lemlist does well

Before we compare alternatives, here's where Lemlist genuinely excels:

**LinkedIn + email multichannel.** Lemlist's Multichannel Expert plan combines email sequences with LinkedIn connection requests, profile visits, and messages. For teams where LinkedIn is a primary channel, this integration is valuable.

**Visual sequence builder.** Lemlist's drag-and-drop sequence builder is one of the most intuitive in the market. You can see the entire flow, add conditional branches, and preview each step visually.

**AI sequence generation.** Lemlist's AI can generate entire sequences from a brief description. Quality is decent — better than generic ChatGPT prompts, though not as good as signal-based personalization.

**Image and video personalization.** Lemlist pioneered personalized images and videos in cold email. Adding a prospect's name to an image or creating a custom video thumbnail. This is a unique feature.

## When Lemlist is the right choice

Stay with Lemlist if:

- **LinkedIn outreach is critical.** If 30%+ of your replies come from LinkedIn sequences, Lemlist's multichannel is hard to replace. No other tool matches their LinkedIn + email integration.
- **You're a small team (1-3 people).** At 1-3 seats, Lemlist's pricing is competitive. The per-seat problem only bites at 4+ people.
- **Image/video personalization drives results.** If personalized images or video thumbnails are part of your winning formula, Lemlist is the only platform with this built in.
- **You've built winning sequences.** If your Lemlist sequences are performing at 10%+ reply rates, the switching cost might not be worth the savings.

## When to switch

Consider alternatives if:

- **Your team is growing past 3 people.** At 4+ seats, per-seat pricing starts to hurt. At 5+ seats on Multichannel ($495/mo), it's hard to justify versus flat-rate options.
- **You need managed infrastructure.** Lemlist requires BYO domains and mailboxes. If infrastructure management is eating your time, moving to a platform with managed infrastructure saves 5-10 hours/month.
- **You're paying for enrichment and verification separately.** Lemlist + Apollo + Clay + ZeroBounce + GW = $600-800/mo for a 3-person team. Consolidating saves 40-60%.
- **LinkedIn isn't generating ROI.** If your LinkedIn steps have a &lt;2% response rate, you're paying for multichannel features that aren't delivering. Switch to a platform that invests those dollars in email deliverability and personalization instead.
