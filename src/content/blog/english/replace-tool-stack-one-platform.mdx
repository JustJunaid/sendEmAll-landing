## The migration guide

### Step 1: Export your data (1-2 hours)

- **From Apollo:** Export your saved lists as CSV. Include all fields: name, email, company, title, company size, industry.
- **From Clay:** Export enrichment data as CSV.
- **From Instantly:** Export campaign results — who replied, who bounced, who unsubscribed. You need this to avoid emailing people who already said no.
- **From your CRM:** Export any suppression lists (customers, active deals, DNC).

### Step 2: Import and verify (30 minutes)

Upload your contacts to SendEmAll. The platform will automatically:
- Deduplicate against existing records
- Verify every email address
- Flag catch-all and risky addresses
- Apply your suppression lists

### Step 3: Rebuild your sequences (1-2 hours)

Recreate your email sequences in SendEmAll. If you have high-performing templates from Instantly or Lemlist, bring them over. The sequence builder supports:
- Multi-step follow-ups
- A/B testing
- Conditional branches (different follow-up if opened vs. not opened)
- Variable insertion from enrichment data

### Step 4: Let Warmup Run (14-21 days)

If you're moving to SendEmAll's managed mailboxes (included in your plan), they need to ramp up. During this period, volume is lower than your previous setup.

**Plan for this.** Don't cancel your old tools on Day 1. Run them in parallel during the 2-3 week ramp period. Cancel once your SendEmAll mailboxes are at full capacity.

### Step 5: Monitor and optimize (ongoing)

Your first 30 days on a new platform are a learning period. Watch:
- Deliverability per domain and mailbox
- Bounce rates vs. your previous platform
- Reply rates vs. previous campaigns
- AI personalization quality (spot-check the first 50-100 emails)
