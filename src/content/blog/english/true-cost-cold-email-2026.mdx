## You're not paying $37/mo

Let's get specific. You signed up for a cold email sending tool. Maybe Instantly at $37/mo. Maybe Lemlist at $39/mo. Maybe Saleshandy at $25/mo.

The price looked great. You entered your card. You were ready to send.

Then reality hit.

Where do the contacts come from? You need a lead database. How do you verify emails before sending? You need a verification tool. Who writes the emails? You need AI. Where do the emails send from? You need domains, mailboxes, and DNS records.

That $37/mo tool? It only sends. Everything else costs extra.

## The full cold email stack, priced out

Here's what a real outbound operation looks like in 2026, tool by tool, with actual prices pulled from public pricing pages.

### 1. Sending platform — $25-358/mo

This is your "cheap" tool. The one that got you in the door.

| Tool | Starter Plan | Growth Plan |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| Instantly | $37/mo | $97/mo |
| Lemlist | $39/mo | $69/mo |
| Saleshandy | $25/mo | $74/mo |
| Smartlead | $39/mo | $94/mo |
| Woodpecker | $29/mo | $49/mo |

Most teams outgrow starter plans within 60 days. You need more mailboxes, more sending volume, more features. Budget $50-100/mo realistically.

### 2. Lead database — $49-119/mo

Your sending tool doesn't find potential buyers for you. You need a database.

| Tool | Price | What you get |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Apollo.io | $49/mo (Basic) | 900 credits/mo, limited filters |
| Apollo.io | $79/mo (Professional) | Unlimited credits, better filters |
| Apollo.io | $119/mo (Organization) | Intent data, AI features |
| LinkedIn Sales Navigator | $99/mo | Search only, no export |
| Ocean.io | $49/mo+ | Lookalike audiences |

Most teams start with Apollo Basic and quickly need Professional for the filters and intent data. Budget $79/mo.

### 3. Enrichment — $149-800/mo

A lead database gives you names and emails. Enrichment gives you the context that makes personalization work: tech stack, funding history, hiring patterns, company news.

| Tool | Price | What you get |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Clay | $149/mo (Explorer) | 3,000 credits |
| Clay | $349/mo (Pro) | 12,000 credits |
| Clay | $800/mo (Team) | 60,000 credits |
| Clearbit (now Breeze) | $99/mo+ | Company data |
| ZoomInfo | $15,000+/year | Enterprise enrichment |

Clay is the most popular choice. But at $149/mo for 3,000 credits, you burn through them fast. One enrichment waterfall can use 5-10 credits per contact. That's 300-600 contacts from your $149/mo plan. If you're running real volume, you're on the $349/mo plan minimum.

Budget: $149-349/mo.

### 4. Email verification — $15-40/mo

Sending to bad emails destroys your domain reputation. Verification is not optional.

| Tool | Price | What you get |
|------|-------|-------------|
| ZeroBounce | $15/mo | 2,000 verifications |
| NeverBounce | $16/mo | 2,500 verifications |
| MillionVerifier | $29 (one-time per 10K) | Bulk verification |
| BriteVerify | $40/mo | 5,000 verifications |

Budget: $15-40/mo for ongoing verification.

### 5. AI writing — $20-49/mo

Nobody writes cold emails manually at scale anymore. You need AI to personalize at volume.

| Tool | Price | What you get |
|------|-------|-------------|
| ChatGPT Plus | $20/mo | General AI, you build prompts |
| ChatGPT Pro | $200/mo | More capacity |
| Clay AI | Included with Clay credits | Uses your credit pool |
| Lemlist AI | Included on higher tiers | Basic AI personalization |

Most teams use ChatGPT Plus alongside their enrichment data. Budget $20/mo minimum.

### 6. Email infrastructure — $50-200/mo

This is the cost nobody talks about when they quote you $37/mo.

**Domains:** You need 5-10 separate domains for cold outreach. Never send from your primary domain. Each domain costs $10-15/year. That's $50-150/year, or $4-13/mo.

**Google Workspace mailboxes:** $7.20/user/month. You need at least 15 mailboxes to send meaningful volume (30-50 emails per mailbox per day). That's $108/mo for Google Workspace alone.

**DNS management:** SPF, DKIM, DMARC records for every domain. Free to set up, but 3-5 hours of your time initially and ongoing monitoring.

**Smart Ramp-Up tools:** New mailboxes need gradual sending increases. Tools like MailReach ($25-99/mo) or Warmbox ($15-69/mo) handle this.

Budget: $108/mo (Google Workspace) + $10/mo (domains) + $25/mo (Smart Ramp-Up) = **$143/mo minimum**.

### 7. Time — the cost you can't see on a pricing page

Managing 6+ tools takes time:

- **Exporting/importing data** between tools: 2-3 hours/week
- **Monitoring deliverability** across mailboxes: 1-2 hours/week
- **DNS configuration** and troubleshooting: 2-3 hours/month
- **Tool updates and breaking changes**: 1-2 hours/month
- **Billing management** across 6 subscriptions: 30 min/month

Conservative estimate: **20-30 hours/month** on tool management. At $50/hour (cheap for a sales ops person), that's $1,000-1,500/mo in labor.

The problem isn't quality. It's fragmentation.

## The "but I already have these tools" objection

Fair. Switching costs are real. Here's when staying on your current stack makes sense:

- You've built custom integrations between tools that work perfectly (rare)
- You're on annual contracts with 6+ months remaining
- Your volume is so low that the free tiers of each tool cover you

Here's when it doesn't make sense:

- You're spending more than $400/mo on tools
- You're losing 10+ hours/month to data management
- Your bounce rates are above 3% because verification gaps slip through
