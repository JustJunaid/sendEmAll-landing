# SendEmAll Blog Content Plan

> 50 posts organized by priority tier, keyword targets, and publishing sequence.
> Research date: April 2026

---

## Content Strategy Overview

### Differentiation Angle
Every competitor (Instantly, Lemlist, Saleshandy, Woodpecker, Smartlead) fights over the same SEO keywords with the same content format. SendEmAll's blog will differentiate on:

1. **Cost transparency** — Nobody writes honestly about total outbound costs. We will.
2. **Original platform data** — Competitors cite each other's reports. We publish our own.
3. **Infrastructure education** — Most guides assume technical knowledge. We explain for founders.
4. **Signal-based prospecting** — We can own this emerging category term.
5. **Developer/API content** — Near-zero competition. Wide open.

### Content Format Strategy
- **Flagship pieces (Tier 1):** 2,000-3,000 words, data-backed, linkable assets
- **Guides (Tier 2):** 1,500-2,500 words, comprehensive how-tos
- **Tactical posts (Tier 3):** 800-1,500 words, specific answers to specific questions
- **Industry playbooks (Tier 4):** 1,200-2,000 words, niche audience targeting

### Publishing Sequence
- **Weeks 1-2:** Tier 1 (flagship pieces that establish positioning)
- **Weeks 3-5:** Tier 2 (high-volume keywords with differentiated angles)
- **Weeks 6-8:** Tier 3 (tactical posts, long-tail keywords)
- **Weeks 9-12:** Tier 4 (industry-specific, developer content, fill gaps)

## TIER 1: Flagship Content (Publish First)

These establish SendEmAll's authority and create linkable assets.

### 1. The True Cost of Cold Email in 2026
- **Target keywords:** "cold email cost", "true cost of cold email", "outbound cost breakdown"
- **Intent:** Commercial | **Competition:** Medium | **Priority:** HIGHEST
- **Angle:** Break down: sending tool ($37-97) + leads ($49-299) + verification ($29-99) + domains ($50-100) + mailboxes ($72-192) + warmup ($49-99) = $600+. Then show SendEmAll at $149 all-in.
- **Why it wins:** Nobody does this honestly. Instantly published "hidden costs of competitors" but it's biased. A brand-agnostic breakdown with a clear conclusion is the most linkable piece we can create.
- **CTA:** Interactive cost calculator on /pricing

### 2. Cold Email Benchmarks 2026: Data From Our Platform
- **Target keywords:** "cold email benchmarks 2026", "cold email statistics", "cold email open rates", "cold email response rate"
- **Intent:** Informational | **Competition:** High (Instantly, Saleshandy dominate) | **Priority:** HIGHEST
- **Angle:** Publish anonymized SendEmAll platform data — open rates, reply rates, bounce rates, best send times, by industry. Instantly's benchmark report gets cited everywhere. We create our own.
- **Why it wins:** Original data = backlinks. Every blog post about cold email needs a stat to cite. Be that source.

### 3. Cold Email Infrastructure Setup: The Complete Guide
- **Target keywords:** "cold email infrastructure", "email infrastructure setup", "domain setup cold email"

- **Why it wins:** Most infrastructure guides assume technical knowledge. A "explain it to a founder" approach fills a gap. Also positions SendEmAll's managed infrastructure as the obvious alternative to DIY.
