> Style: Dark theme, clean motion graphics, screen recording feel.
> Music: Lo-fi electronic, subtle, no vocals. Think Linear or Vercel product videos.

## Production Notes

### Visual Style
- **Color palette:** Dark background (#0f0a1e → #1a122e), lavender accents (#D2B3F3), teal for positive metrics (#14B8A6), coral for competitor prices (#EF4444)
- **Typography:** Sora font throughout (matches website)
- **Motion:** Smooth easing, no jarring cuts. Everything slides, fades, or morphs. Think Apple product videos.
- **UI mockups:** Should look like a real product dashboard, not obviously fake. Use the same dark theme as the website.

### What NOT to Do
- No talking head / AI avatar — this is a product demo, not a webinar
- No stock footage of "business people shaking hands"
- No cheesy email flying animations
- No voiceover (text on screen is cleaner for this length)
- No feature list scroll — show, don't tell

### Aspect Ratio & Format
- **16:9** for desktop hero (primary)
- **1:1** crop for social media sharing
- **MP4, H.264**, target 5-8MB for web (compress with HandBrake)
- Aim for 30fps, 1080p minimum

## AI Video Tool Recommendations

### Option A: Screen Recording + Motion Graphics (BEST FOR AUTHENTICITY)

| Step | Tool | Cost | What It Does |
|------|------|------|-------------|
| 1. Record product UI | **Loom** or **OBS Studio** | Free | Capture real (or Figma mockup) dashboard |
| 2. Motion graphics | **CapCut Pro** | $8/mo | Text animations, transitions, split screens |
| 3. Polish | **Runway Gen-3 Alpha** | $15/mo | Smooth transitions, background generation |
| 4. Music | **Artlist** or **Epidemic Sound** | $10-15/mo | Royalty-free lo-fi electronic |

**Total: ~$25-40/mo. Best result if you have product UI or Figma mockups.**

### Option B: Full AI-Generated (FASTEST)

| Tool | Cost | Strength | Weakness |
|------|------|----------|----------|
| **Synthesia** | $29/mo | Professional templates, screen recording overlays | Avatar-based, can feel generic |
| **HeyGen** | $29/mo | Most realistic AI avatars, lip sync | Still recognizable as AI by sharp eyes |
| **Runway Gen-3** | $15/mo | Best cinematic quality, custom generation | Harder to control exact product UI shots |
| **Pika** | $10/mo | Good for short motion clips | Less control over complex scenes |

**My pick: Synthesia or HeyGen for the text-overlay style (no avatar needed for this script). Use their screen recording overlay feature with Figma mockups of your dashboard.**

### Option C: Hire It Out

| Service | Cost | Timeline |
|---------|------|----------|
| **Fiverr** (SaaS product video) | $200-500 | 3-5 days |
| **VideoHusky** | $600-1,200/mo | 2-3 videos/month |
| **Vidico** | $3-8K one-time | 2-3 weeks, agency quality |

**For a hero video that's this important to conversions, Option A (record + CapCut) gives the most authentic result. Option C (Fiverr $300-500 range) is worth it if you want polish without the learning curve.**

## How to Integrate Into the Site

The Upstart template already has a video modal button in HomeBanner. To enable it:

1. Upload the video to a hosting service (YouTube unlisted, Vimeo, or self-host on Vercel)
2. Update `src/content/sections/english/home-banner.md`:

```yaml
buttons:
  - enable: true
    label: "Start Free — 100 Credits"
    url: "https://app.sendemall.com/signup"
  - enable: true
    label: "Watch Demo"
    type: "video"
    video:
      id: "demo-video"
      src: "https://www.youtube.com/embed/YOUR_VIDEO_ID"
```

The modal, play button animation, and overlay are already built into HomeBanner.astro.
