Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2024

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferred language and other settings, which can make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

2. How We Use Cookies

SendEmAll uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Provide personalized content and features
  • Analyze how our website is used
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Deliver relevant advertisements

We may use third-party services that place cookies on your device. These services include:

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about your use of our website, including your IP address. This information is transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States.

We use social media platforms like Facebook, LinkedIn, and Twitter for advertising and analytics purposes. These platforms may place cookies on your device when you visit our website.

When you make a payment, our payment processors (Stripe, PayPal) may place cookies to ensure secure transactions and prevent fraud.

5. Cookie Management

5.1 Browser Settings

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete cookies individually or all at once
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies
  • Set preferences for different types of cookies

5.2 Browser-Specific Instructions

Chrome

Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data

Firefox

Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

Edge

Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

5.3 Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner that allows you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in our footer.

6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you choose to disable cookies, some features of our website may not function properly:

  • You may need to log in every time you visit
  • Your preferences and settings won't be remembered
  • Some personalized content may not be available
  • Analytics and performance monitoring may be limited
  • Security features may be affected

7. Do Not Track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that sends a signal to websites indicating that you don't want to be tracked. Currently, there is no standard for how websites should respond to DNT signals. We do not currently respond to DNT signals, but we respect your privacy choices through our cookie consent mechanism.
